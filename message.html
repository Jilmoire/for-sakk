I'm not the type to initiate but I just did what I wanted to do: I liked some of your IG stories, heart reacted some notes, and felt something I ususally wont do for someone I'm not even close with - worrying and checking in (worried friend type shii). I was happy with that progress but there's this sense of deficiency or whatever that was. The important thing is that the awkward phase ended. If you think about it, it's kind of funny. You have this guy who you barely knew suddenly wanting a close relationship. "Wow, is this even me?" I thought at first. "F*ck it, I'm just sending it." then suddenly you have someone asking for your credit card pin number. "Oh, is that so? I didn't know you liked this." brother I DIDN'T KNOW I COULD TALK TO SOMEONE LIKE THIS?!? HELLO?!?!? HELP????? "How's your day?", "Were you busy?" I was surpised that you just went with it, but I'm very grateful that you stayed. Again, congratulations. Much love, Guilmar
